Which car is the best fit for YOU?



There are a lot of cars and everyone needs a different car for a different reason. Some factors that affect car choices might be the location where you live, fuel efficiency, or budget ranges. By investigating the average MPG (miles per gallon) value for highway and city of various car brands in 2017, this narrative visualization will guide you to decide which car is the best fit for you.
